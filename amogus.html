I Am Scared of The Red Impostor

Amogus Tale

Written By Blue Crewmate

The Impostor Rose

The red impostor came from below, the knife in his hand and blood of its edge
tells the history of this legendary monster who roamed the Earth ages ago.

As he opened his eyes and stared at the great horizon of what was, he has now seen...
what has. He decided to venture and walk around for he has not moved his legs for decades
He has once again, walked among us.
